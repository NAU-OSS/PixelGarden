# PixelGarden Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in PixelGarden a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards
Community members are expected to:
- Be respectful and considerate in all communications and interactions
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and project
- Show empathy and patience toward others
- Communicate openly and inclusively

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory comments
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting language
- Public or private harassment of any community member
- Sharing inappropriate, offensive, or explicit content
- Deliberate disruption of project spaces or discussions

---

## Enforcement Guidelines
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Actions taken may include:
- A private warning from maintainers
- Temporary suspension of participation or contribution privileges
- Permanent removal from project spaces
- Reporting to appropriate authorities in case of legal threats

Enforcement decisions will be made at the discretion of project maintainers, considering the severity and frequency of the offense. All actions will be documented and reviewed to ensure fairness.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repository and issues
- Pull requests and discussions
- Communication platforms associated with the project (e.g., Discord, Slack, forums)
- Public spaces where individuals are representing PixelGarden

---

## Our Values
PixelGarden community values:
- Openness: welcoming all contributions and perspectives
- Respect: treating everyone with kindness and understanding
- Collaboration: working together to improve the project
- Accountability: taking responsibility for actions and decisions
