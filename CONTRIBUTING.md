Welcome! PixelGarden is an open-source, community-driven project. We are excited to have you contribute, whether it's fixing bugs, adding new features, improving documentation, or helping other contributors. By contributing, you agree to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## How to Contribute

### 1. Issues
- Check if an issue already exists before creating a new one.
- Use clear titles and descriptions.
- Label issues appropriately (`bug`, `enhancement`, `good first issue`).
- To report a bug, include:
  - Steps to reproduce
  - Screenshots if applicable
  - Browser or environment details
